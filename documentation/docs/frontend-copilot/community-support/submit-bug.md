# Submit a bug or request

We are committed to providing a seamless and efficient design-to-code conversion experience. However, we understand that despite our rigorous testing, bugs can slip through the cracks.

This is where you can play a crucial role. Not only do you help us enhance the quality of Bitloops, but you also get rewarded for your contributions.

## üéÅ Spot, report, reward program

Here's how you can participate and earn free credits:

### 1. Identifying Bugs

Begin by using Bitloops as you normally would and keep an eye out for:

- Unexpected behavior
- Glitches or visual issues
- Inconsistencies in output
- Performance problems

Whether it's a minor interface issue or a major functionality flaw, your keen observations are invaluable.

### 2. Generating a Bug Report

If you encounter a bug, prepare to document it:

| Include | Why It Helps |
|---------|--------------|
| **What you were doing** | Provides context |
| **Steps to reproduce** | Helps us recreate the issue |
| **Expected vs. actual results** | Clarifies the problem |
| **Screenshots or video** | Visual evidence speeds up diagnosis |

:::tip Pro Tip
A short video recording is the most helpful way to demonstrate an issue. Tools like Loom or screen recording make this easy.
:::

### 3. Submitting Your Report

**Option 1: Email**
Send your report to [support@bitloops.com](mailto:support@bitloops.com) with:
- Detailed description
- Steps to reproduce
- Screenshots or video attachment
- Your contact information

**Option 2: Discord**
Post in our [Discord #help channel](https://discord.gg/vj8EdZx8gK) for community visibility and faster response.

### 4. Earning Your Credits

Once your bug report is submitted, our team will:

1. Review the details
2. Verify the issue
3. Credit your Bitloops account

These credits can be used for:

- Future subscriptions
- Additional features
- Conversion credits within Bitloops

## Feature Requests

Have an idea for improving Bitloops? We'd love to hear it!

**How to submit:**

1. Join our [Discord](https://discord.gg/vj8EdZx8gK)
2. Post in the #feedback channel
3. Describe your idea and how it would help your workflow

We review all feature requests and prioritize based on community feedback.
