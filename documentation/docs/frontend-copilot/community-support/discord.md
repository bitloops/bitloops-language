# Join Our Discord Community

Our community lives on [Discord](https://discord.gg/vj8EdZx8gK) — it's the fastest way to connect with the Bitloops team and other developers.

## What You'll Find

| Channel | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **#general** | General discussions about Bitloops and frontend development |
| **#help** | Get help with issues and questions |
| **#feedback** | Share your thoughts and feature requests |
| **#showcase** | Show off what you've built with Bitloops |
| **#announcements** | Stay updated on new features and releases |

## Why Join?

- **Get Help Fast:** Ask questions and get answers from the Bitloops team and community members. We're active and ready to help!
- **Share Feedback:** Your input shapes the future of Bitloops. Share feature requests, report issues, and help us build a better tool.
- **See What Others Are Building:** Get inspired by what other developers are creating with Bitloops. Share your own projects and get feedback.
- **Early Access:** Discord members often get early access to new features and the opportunity to provide feedback before public releases.
- **Direct Access to the Team:** The Bitloops team is active on Discord. Get direct answers from the people building the product.

---

## Community Guidelines

To keep our community helpful and welcoming:

- **Be respectful** — Treat everyone with kindness
- **Stay on topic** — Use appropriate channels for your messages
- **Search first** — Check if your question has been answered before
- **Share knowledge** — Help others when you can
- **No spam** — Keep promotions relevant and minimal
